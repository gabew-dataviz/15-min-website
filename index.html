Star Wars - Disney Acquisition

Gabriel Wolofsky MAS.S70 Demonstration Project

Researcher | MIT Transit Lab | wolofsky@mit.edu

Since Disney purchased the Star Wars franchise in 2012, the number of co-occurances in news articles has increased dramatically. In terms of normalized 2001 mentions, co-occurances peaked at nearly 14x their base rate. Although the Phi-coefficient also increased dramatically during this period, more than 10x, the peak value of 0.2 does still not show a strong correlation. This is due to the multiplicity of uses for the words "star" and "wars", and because of Disney's many franchises.
